Crystal Collector Game!

You will be provided with a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals, each with their own specific value. By clicking on a crystal, you will add that specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly generated number, but you will lose the game if your total score exceeds that of the random number.

The value of each crystal will remain unknown until you click on it.

Both the random number and value of each crystal will change with the start of each new game.
